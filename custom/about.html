Declutter is the ultimate After Effects extension for those who crave order in their projects! With Declutter, your assets practically organize themselves.

Create, tweak, and save as many custom folder structures as you need for any kind of project.

Customize everything—from folder names and color labels to the powerful new filters that make sorting a breeze.

When you're ready, just pick a structure, and watch your project panel transform in seconds!

Tip: Want to organize only specific assets? Select the assets and folders you want to target. To sort everything, just deselect all.

Visit https://aescripts.com/declutter for more.
